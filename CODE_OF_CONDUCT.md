# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone,
regardless of experience level, identity, background, or personal
characteristics.

Be respectful, assume good intent, give actionable feedback, and focus
discussion on the project. Harassment, threats, discrimination, sustained
disruption, and publication of another person's private information are not
acceptable.

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior harms the community. Reports
will be handled as privately and fairly as practical. For sensitive conduct
reports, use the repository's private security reporting flow; if unavailable,
request a private contact channel without posting details publicly.

This policy is informed by the
[Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
